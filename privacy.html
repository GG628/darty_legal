Privacy Policy for Darty

Last Updated: January 17, 2026

Darty ("we", "our", or "us") provides a darts scoring and statistics application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Data Collection

Personal Data: Darty is designed to work primarily offline. We do not require you to create an account or provide an email address to use the core features of the app.

Device Information: We may collect basic, non-identifying technical data required for app functionality, such as your device type and OS version, which is standard for apps built on the Expo platform.

2. Game Data

Your game scores, statistics, and tournament progress are stored locally on your device. We do not upload your personal game history to our servers unless you explicitly use a backup or sync feature (if provided in future updates).

3. Third-Party Services

We use standard mobile platform services which may collect data according to their own policies:

  • Apple App Store / Google Play: For installation and analytics.
  • Expo: To provide over-the-air updates and crash reporting.

4. Advertising and Tracking

Darty does not include third-party advertising or "tracking" pixels designed to follow you across other websites or apps for marketing purposes.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
