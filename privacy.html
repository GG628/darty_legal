Privacy Policy for Darty

Last Updated: January 23, 2026

Darty ("we", "our", or "us") provides a darts scoring and statistics application. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

1. Overview

Darty is designed to work primarily offline and does not require you to create an account or sign in. All game data, statistics, and user profiles are stored locally on your device. We do not operate a cloud database or store your personal information on our servers.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Game Data (Stored Locally Only)

The following data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Player names and profiles
  • Game scores and statistics
  • Tournament results and history
  • Custom game settings and preferences
  • Achievement progress

Storage Location: Local SQLite database on your device
Access: Only you have access to this data
Deletion: Delete the app or use "Reset Everything" in App Settings

2.2 Crash Reports & Diagnostics

We use Sentry (a third-party service) to collect crash reports and diagnostic data when the app encounters errors. This helps us identify and fix bugs.

What We Collect:

  • Crash logs and stack traces (technical error details)
  • Device information: model, operating system version, screen resolution
  • App version and build number
  • Breadcrumb data: sequence of user actions leading to a crash (e.g., "navigated to Stats screen ‚Üí clicked Tournament ‚Üí crash")
  • Performance data: screen load times, frame rates, slow operations

What We DO NOT Collect:

  • Your name, email, or any personally identifying information
  • Your game data, scores, or statistics
  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, or other personal files

Purpose: Fix bugs, improve app stability, and enhance performance
Data Controller: Sentry (Functional Software, Inc.)
Data Retention: Crash data is retained for 90 days, then automatically deleted
Privacy Policy: https://sentry.io/privacy/

Important: Crash data is not linked to your identity. We cannot identify who experienced a crash, only what went wrong technically.

2.3 Installation ID

We collect a unique installation identifier to deliver app updates:

  • Purpose: Track app installations for over-the-air updates (via Expo)
  • Usage: Deliver bug fixes and feature updates without requiring App Store downloads
  • Data Type: Anonymous installation ID (not linked to your identity)

2.4 Technical Data for App Functionality

We collect minimal technical data required for the app to function:

  • Device model and operating system version (for compatibility testing)
  • App version (to ensure you receive appropriate updates)
  • Timezone (for accurate timestamp display in game history)

Purpose: Ensure app compatibility and deliver updates
Storage: Processed by Expo and Google Play Services

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

3.1 Sentry (Crash Reporting)

  • Purpose: Crash reporting and performance monitoring
  • Data Collected: Crash logs, device info, performance metrics (see Section 2.2)
  • Privacy Policy: https://sentry.io/privacy/
  • Data Location: United States (complies with GDPR)

3.2 Expo (App Updates & Monitoring)

  • Purpose: Deliver over-the-air updates and monitor app stability
  • Data Collected: Installation ID, device model, OS version, app version
  • Privacy Policy: https://expo.dev/privacy
  • Data Location: United States

3.3 EAS Insights (Analytics)

  • Purpose: Understand app usage patterns and improve user experience
  • Data Collected: Session duration, app opens/closes, device model, OS version, app version
  • What We Track: How often the app is used, session lengths, which app versions are active
  • What We DON'T Track: Your game data, scores, personal information, or specific in-app actions
  • Privacy Policy: https://expo.dev/privacy
  • Data Location: United States

Important: Analytics data is not linked to your identity. We see aggregate metrics like "500 users opened the app today" but cannot identify individual users.

3.4 Google Play Services

  • Purpose: Core Android functionality, app distribution, and in-app billing
  • Data Collected: Device information, app usage data (managed by Google)
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. How We Use Your Data

Data Type Purpose
Game Data (Local) Store your scores, stats, and preferences on your device
Crash Reports Identify and fix bugs to improve app stability
Performance Data Optimize app speed and responsiveness
Installation ID Deliver over-the-air updates
Technical Data Ensure compatibility across devices and OS versions
Analytics Data Understand usage patterns and improve app experience

We DO NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Use your data for advertising or marketing
  • Share your data with anyone except the third-party services listed above (and only for the purposes described)
  • Track your location
  • Access your contacts, photos, or other personal files

5. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your data. We only share data with:

  1. Service Providers: Sentry, Expo, and EAS Insights (as described in Section 3)
  2. Legal Requirements: We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or government request
  3. Business Transfers: If Darty is acquired or merged, your data may transfer to the new owner (you will be notified)

6. Data Retention & Deletion

6.1 Local Game Data

  • Retention: Stored on your device indefinitely until you delete it
  • Deletion Options:
    1. Uninstall the app ‚Üí All data is permanently deleted
    2. App Settings ‚Üí Reset Everything ‚Üí Clears all local data without uninstalling
    3. App Settings ‚Üí Clear Stats ‚Üí Deletes game history while keeping player profiles

6.2 Crash Reports (Sentry)

  • Retention: 90 days, then automatically deleted
  • Deletion: Contact us at danrush730@gmail.com to request immediate deletion

6.3 Analytics Data (EAS Insights)

  • Retention: Retained as long as your app is active, aggregated data only
  • Deletion: Contact us at danrush730@gmail.com to request deletion

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (see Section 6)
  • Objection: Object to certain data processing activities
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format (for locally stored data, export from App Settings)

To exercise these rights, contact us at: danrush730@gmail.com

8. International Data Transfers

Darty is developed in the United States. Your data may be processed and stored in the United States and other countries where our service providers operate (Sentry, Expo, EAS Insights).

These services comply with GDPR and other international privacy regulations through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Privacy Shield frameworks (where applicable)
  • Adequate data protection measures

9. Children's Privacy

Darty is a general audience app suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years old.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at danrush730@gmail.com, and we will delete it promptly.

10. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data:

  • Local game data is stored securely on your device using Android's app sandboxing
  • Data transmitted to third-party services (Sentry, Expo, EAS Insights) is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • We use industry-standard security practices for all integrations

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do:

  1. The "Last Updated" date at the top will change
  2. We will notify you via an in-app notice or update notes
  3. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy

Your Responsibility: Review this policy periodically for changes
