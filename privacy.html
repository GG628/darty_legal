Privacy Policy for Darty

Last Updated: January 22, 2026

Darty ("we", "our", or "us") provides a darts scoring and statistics application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Data Collection & Usage

1.1 Personal Data & Local Storage

Darty is designed to work primarily offline. We do not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or sign in to use the app. All game data, statistics, and user profiles are stored locally on your device.

1.2 Device Information

We may collect basic, non-identifying technical data required for app functionality (e.g., device model, operating system version) to assist with compatibility and crash reporting.

1.3 Identifiers (Android Advertising ID / App Set ID)

The application uses third-party services that may collect device identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID or App Set ID) for fraud prevention, security, and subscription management purposes. We do not use these identifiers for behavioral advertising or marketing tracking.

2. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

  • RevenueCat: Used to manage subscription entitlements and purchase receipts.
  • Expo: Used for over-the-air updates and monitoring app stability.
  • Google Play Services: Used for core Android functionality.

3. Subscriptions & Financial Data

We use RevenueCat to facilitate potential in-app purchases. While the app may currently be free, the infrastructure is present to verify purchase receipts.

  • We do not process or store your credit card or financial information.
  • All transactions are processed securely by the Google Play Store.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

Google Play policy requires us to clearly explain how you can delete your data.

  • Retention: Since we do not run a cloud database, your data exists only as long as the app is installed on your device.
  • Deletion: You can delete all your data at any time by uninstalling the application. Alternatively, you can use the "Reset Everything" or "Clear All Stats" options found within the App Settings to wipe data without uninstalling.
